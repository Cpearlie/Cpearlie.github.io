Jin

Full Name : Kim Seok Jin (김석진)
Position : Vocalist, Visual
Birthday :December 4, 1992
Birthplace :Gwacheon, Gyeonggi Province, South Korea
How was Jin discovered? :Kim Seok Jin was discovered on the street while in university studying acting, and was invited to audition for BigHit Entertainment. When BTS dropped their first album in 2013, Jin debuted as one of the group’s four vocalists.
