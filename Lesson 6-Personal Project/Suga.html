Suga

Full Name :Min Yoon Gi (민윤기)
Position : Lead Rapper
Birthday :March 9, 1993
Birthplace :Buk-gu, Daegu, South Korea
How was Suga discovered? : Suga had a career as an underground rapper before getting into the K-pop game. Suga had begun to establish himself as a producer, composing beats for local artists in his hometown of Daegu, primarily for a group called “D-TOWN.” When BTS dropped their first album in 2013, Min debuted as one of the group’s three rappers under the stage name Suga.
